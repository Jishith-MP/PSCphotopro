This Privacy Policy will help you better understand how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

Privacy Policy

The PSCphotopro website is designed to facilitate the downloading of photographs and autographs required for Kerala PSC registration, in accordance with specified requirements. We utilize the HTML5 Canvas API to provide these services, ensuring that all processes occur directly on the client side.
Please be assured that we do not collect or store any personal information, including photographs, signatures, dates, or names. All data processing happens entirely on your device. We provide a client-side script to handle these operations, guaranteeing that your privacy and personal data are fully protected.

Technology and hosting

Our website utilizes Bootstrap for styling to ensure a modern and responsive design. For image hosting, we exclusively use Imgbb. We do not employ any unauthorized third-party websites or services.

We are committed to ensuring that we do not take on any risk associated with the storage or misuse of user data. While we strive to meet user requirements and specifications to the best of our ability, we do not guarantee that all exact requirements will be fulfilled.
We continuously work to stay updated with the latest requirements and specifications. For detailed information and examples of how our service meets these requirements, please refer to the "Example Demonstration" section accessible via the navigation menu.
